# ❤️ Diabetes Risk Prediction – ANOVA & Regression  

**Goal:** To determine how Age, BMI Index, and Glucose Level influence diabetes risk.  

**Tools:** R, Python, Regression, ANOVA  

**What I Did:**  
- Conducted statistical tests to analyze relationships between health metrics and risk levels.  
- Evaluated significance using ANOVA and regression coefficients.  

**Key Insight:**  
Glucose level and BMI were found to be the most impactful factors in diabetes prediction.
